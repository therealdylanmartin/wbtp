Donate Books

Wisconsin Books to Prisoners (WBTP) needs your lightly-used books! The range of interests prisoners have in the world is as broad and varied as the population at large. Donations of books across the entire spectrum of fiction and nonfiction subjects are both needed and appreciated.

Books by and about African Americans and books about occupational trades and how to start a business are frequently requested. History books, reference books, books on learning languages, as well as books on how to draw are also in high demand.

Please note:

WBTP can only accept new and gently-used paperback books with no handwriting, margin notes, or highlighting.
