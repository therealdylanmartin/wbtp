Donate

At Wisconsin Books to Prisoners (WBTP), our primary duty is to respond to requests for books from prisoners throughout the state. Many prison libraries are in a a state of disrepair, with an insufficient supply of modern, useful books. Without your donated books and financial contributions, WBTP would not be able to fulfill its core mission.

All monetary donations go to postage and the purchase of collegiate dictionaries. It costs roughly $3.50 to send a package of 3 books to a prisoner. The number of requests for books WBTP can fulfill depends entirely on the generosity of people like you.
